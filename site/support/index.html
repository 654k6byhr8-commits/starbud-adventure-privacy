Starbud Adventure Support

Support page for closed testing and store review.

During TestFlight or closed beta testing, please use the platform beta feedback tool to report crashes, control issues, audio problems, unreadable UI, balance issues, or other gameplay problems.

What To Include

  • Device model and operating system version.
  • Build version or TestFlight build number.
  • What you were doing before the issue happened.
  • Screenshots or screen recordings when available.
