## 2. Data We May Collect

Depending on the test build and enabled backend features, the app may collect or process:

- Account identifiers such as player ID, email address, or sign-in identifier if login is enabled.
- Gameplay data such as character name, class mode, skill allocation, equipment, map progress, kills, deaths, revives, scores, and leaderboard values.
- Purchase verification data such as platform, product ID, transaction ID, purchase token, and verification result if in-app purchases are enabled.
- Anti-cheat and session data such as combat session ID, monster kill events, timestamps, and suspicious activity flags.
- Diagnostics such as app version, device model, crash logs, performance data, and error logs when diagnostics or crash reporting are enabled.

## 3. How We Use Data

- Operate gameplay, save progress, and restore player state.
- Verify purchases and prevent duplicate or fraudulent transactions.
- Protect leaderboards and gameplay balance from cheating or abuse.
- Investigate crashes, bugs, support requests, and closed-test feedback.
- Improve stability, controls, audio, art readability, and combat balance.

## 4. Sharing And Third Parties

We do not sell personal data. These providers are used only for app distribution, operation, security, diagnostics, support, or purchase verification.
